Dino Buddies With Mashed Potato Volcano

Description

Take your dinner to a prehistoric level with this fun and engaging Dino Buddies With Mashed Potato Volcano recipe! Perfect for kids and adults alike, this dish combines crispy dinosaur-shaped chicken nuggets with a creamy, buttery mashed potato "volcano," complete with a delicious gravy "lava" flow. It's not just a mealâ€”it's an adventure on a plate!


Ingredients

  • 16 pieces Yummy Dino Buddies
  • 12 oz fresh broccoli florets
  • 24 oz mashed potatoes
  • 12 oz gravy

Instructions

  1. Prepare Dino Buddies according to directions on package.
  2. While Dino Buddies are baking prepare the rest of the ingredients.
  3. When Dino Buddies are done baking remove from oven and place on plate.
  4. Scoop prepared mashed potatoes onto plate and form into volcano shape with indent on top. Top the mashed potatoes with gravy for lava and place broccoli florets around the base of mashed potatoes for trees.
